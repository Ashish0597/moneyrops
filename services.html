Before You Apply

Eligibility & documents

Requirements vary slightly by product, but most applications move faster when these four areas are sorted in advance. Our team confirms the exact checklist for your specific facility on the first call.

Who can apply

Individuals, salaried professionals, proprietorships, partnerships, and private limited companies age and income criteria vary by product.

KYC documents

PAN, Aadhaar, address proof, and recent photographs for all applicants and co-applicants/guarantors where applicable.

Income & bank proof

Last 6–12 months' bank statements, ITRs, salary slips or GST returns, depending on whether you're salaried or self-employed.

Collateral papers

For secured facilities property title deeds, approved plans, or stock/receivable statements as relevant to the loan type.
